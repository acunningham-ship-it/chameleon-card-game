How it works

Three steps. One winner.

No long rulebook, no setup headache. Deal the cards and you're playing in under two minutes.

1

Get your color

Every player gets a hand of cards in a mix of colors. Your mission: turn them all into your color.

2

Play & swap

Use Play Cards to change colors, swap hands, skip rivals, and flip the table when they least expect it.

3

Match to win

First to turn their whole hand to a single color takes the round. Best of the night takes bragging rights.
